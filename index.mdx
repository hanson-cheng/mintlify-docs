## Welcome to Prezence AI

Prezence AI is a powerful LinkedIn lead generation platform that combines advanced conversation management with unlimited customizable AI automation. Our platform enables you to create unique AI agents tailored to specific offers, ideal customer profiles, and sales processes, allowing you to scale personalized outreach like never before.
