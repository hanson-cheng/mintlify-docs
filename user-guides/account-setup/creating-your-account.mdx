Welcome aboard! Creating your Prezence AI account is the first step towards automating your lead generation. This guide will walk you through the process.

## Step 1: Visit the Registration Page

Open your web browser and navigate to the Prezence AI registration page.

[**Go to Registration**](https://app.prezence.ai/auth/register)

## Step 2: Fill in Your Details

You'll see a simple sign-up form. Please provide the following information:

- **Full Name**: Your first and last name.
- **Email Address**: This will be your username and where we send important notifications.
- **Password**: Choose a strong, secure password.

<Card>
  **Password Tip:** We recommend using a password manager to generate and store a unique password for Prezence AI.
</Card>

## Step 3: Agree to Terms and Sign Up

Once you've filled in your details, read through our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. If you agree, check the box and click the **"Sign Up"** button.

## Step 4: Verify Your Email Address

For your security, we need to verify that you own the email address you provided. 

- Check your inbox for an email from `support@prezence.ai` with the subject line "Verify Your Email Address".
- Open the email and click the **"Verify Email"** button.

<Warning>
  If you don't see the email within a few minutes, please check your spam or junk folder. If it's still not there, you can request a new verification email from the login page.
</Warning>

## Step 5: Log In

After verifying your email, you'll be redirected to the login page. Enter your email and password to log in to your new Prezence AI account for the first time.

Congratulations! Your account is now active. The next step is to [Connect Your LinkedIn Account](/user-guides/account-setup/connecting-linkedin) to start importing leads.
